Sankt Pauli

Today was the one year anniversary of Kandie Gang Social Rides, and let me tell you, it was a quite a ride. We grew from 11 riders to over 50 today. Although we have grown, the Kandie Gang ethos and purpose remain the same:

It’s about Streetlife.

It’s about Urbanity.

It’s about Gettin Together.

It’s about Having Fun.

It’s about Riding.

It’s about Adventure.

It’s about Gettin Out.

It’s about Freedom.

It’s about Nature.

It’s a Lovestory.

no gender. no race. no ignorance. no hate. just love. just tolerance. just peace.

Join us on our next ride.
