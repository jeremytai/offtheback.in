S-Cup / Bad Oldesloe

S-Cup / Bad Oldesloe

Today was the 3rd race in the Specialized-Cup Series and my first criterium in Germany. I signed-up thinking I’d like to see how my fitness is leading into the summer months and also to see what crit racing was like in Hamburg.

Bad Oldesloe was the same location where there was one of the more demanding cyclocross races from last year. With punchy climbs and some extremely fast downhills leading into fast turns, the criterium didn’t disappoint. Thank goodness it remained dry as crashing in a road race means bike damage, road rash or something much worse.
